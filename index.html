About Me

I am a senior at Olin College of Engineering in Needham, MA. I'll graduate in May with my BS in electrical and computer engineering.

Through my project-based education I have experience doing embedded systems prototyping, firmware development, and user-centered design on student projects, in internships, and on my Formula SAE Electric team. I am hoping to find a career bringing ideas and interactions to life with electronics in the aerospace, automotive or consumer electronics industries. In my free time I run a tiny coffee shop!
